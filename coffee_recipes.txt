Customer: Can you recommend a classic coffee drink?
Barista: Certainly! How about a Classic Cappuccino? Here's the recipe:
Recipe: Classic Cappuccino
Ingredients:
- 1 shot espresso
- 2 oz steamed milk
- 2 oz milk foam
Instructions:
1. Pull a shot of espresso into a cappuccino cup.
2. Steam the milk, creating microfoam.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso.
4. Top with milk foam.
5. Serve immediately.

Customer: I'm in the mood for something cold and sweet.
Barista: I'd recommend our Iced Caramel Latte. It's perfect for a sweet, refreshing treat. Here's how we make it:
Recipe: Iced Caramel Latte
Ingredients:
- 2 shots espresso
- 4 oz cold milk
- 1 oz caramel syrup
- Ice cubes
Instructions:
1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
2. Pour caramel syrup over the ice.
3. Add cold milk.
4. Pour espresso shots over the milk.
5. Stir gently and serve.

Customer: Do you have any non-coffee options?
Barista: Absolutely! Our Matcha Green Tea Latte is a popular non-coffee choice. Here's the recipe:
Recipe: Matcha Green Tea Latte
Ingredients:
- 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
- 1 oz hot water
- 8 oz steamed milk
- 1 tsp honey (optional)
Instructions:
1. Whisk matcha powder with hot water in a cup until smooth.
2. Steam the milk until frothy.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the matcha mixture.
4. Add honey if desired and stir gently.
5. Serve hot.

Customer: I love chocolate. Do you have a chocolatey coffee drink?
Barista: You're in luck! Our Mocha is perfect for chocolate lovers. Here's how we prepare it:
Recipe: Mocha
Ingredients:
- 2 shots espresso
- 6 oz steamed milk
- 1 oz chocolate syrup
- Whipped cream (optional)
Instructions:
1. Pour chocolate syrup into a mug.
2. Add espresso shots and stir to combine.
3. Steam the milk until frothy.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso and chocolate mixture, stirring gently.
5. Top with whipped cream if desired and serve hot.

Customer: I'd like something with a unique flavor. Any suggestions?
Barista: How about our Lavender London Fog? It's a fragrant and soothing tea-based drink. Here's the recipe:
Recipe: Lavender London Fog
Ingredients:
- 1 Earl Grey tea bag
- 4 oz hot water
- 4 oz steamed milk
- 1/2 oz lavender syrup
- 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions:
1. Steep Earl Grey tea bag in hot water for 3-5 minutes.
2. Remove tea bag and stir in lavender syrup and vanilla extract.
3. Steam the milk until frothy.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the tea mixture.
5. Serve hot.

Customer: It's a hot day. What's a good cold coffee option?
Barista: On a hot day, our Cold Brew Coffee is an excellent choice. It's smooth and refreshing. Here's how we make it:
Recipe: Cold Brew Coffee
Ingredients:
- 1 cup coarsely ground coffee
- 4 cups cold water
Instructions:
1. Combine ground coffee and cold water in a large jar.
2. Stir gently to ensure all grounds are wet.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 12-24 hours.
4. Strain the coffee through a fine-mesh sieve lined with cheesecloth.
5. Serve over ice or dilute with water to taste.

Customer: I'm looking for something indulgent. What do you recommend?
Barista: For an indulgent treat, I'd suggest our Salted Caramel Macchiato. It's rich and delicious. Here's the recipe:
Recipe: Salted Caramel Macchiato
Ingredients:
- 2 shots espresso
- 6 oz steamed milk
- 1 oz caramel syrup
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
- Caramel drizzle (optional)
Instructions:
1. Mix caramel syrup and sea salt in a mug.
2. Steam the milk until frothy.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the caramel mixture.
4. Slowly pour espresso shots over the milk.
5. Top with caramel drizzle if desired and serve.

Recipe: Pumpkin Spice Latte
Ingredients:
- 2 shots espresso

Customer: I'm trying to cut back on dairy. What options do you have?
Barista: We have several non-dairy options. One popular choice is our Honey Almond Milk Flat White. Here's the recipe:
Recipe: Honey Almond Milk Flat White
Ingredients:
- 2 shots espresso
- 4 oz steamed almond milk
- 1 tsp honey
Instructions:
1. Stir honey into espresso shots in a small mug.
2. Steam almond milk until it's very hot with small, tight bubbles.
3. Pour the steamed almond milk over the espresso, creating a thin layer of microfoam on top.
4. Serve immediately.

Customer: I love coconut. Do you have any coconut-flavored drinks?
Barista: Absolutely! Our Coconut Mocha Frappé is a delicious coconut-flavored option. Here's how we prepare it:
Recipe: Coconut Mocha Frappé
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup strong coffee, chilled
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
- 2 cups ice
- Coconut whipped cream (optional)
Instructions:
1. Blend chilled coffee, coconut milk, chocolate syrup, and ice in a blender until smooth.
2. Pour into a tall glass.
3. Top with coconut whipped cream if desired.
4. Sprinkle with cocoa powder and serve with a straw.
